AI Integration

How the AI Works

Our game uses PictoBlox's built-in AI extension to enable hands-free, touchless gameplay through real-time hand motion detection.

1
Webcam Detects Your Hand

The computer's webcam captures a live video feed and sends it to PictoBlox's AI vision module in real time.

2
Hand Pose Recognition

The AI identifies key hand landmarks and determines the position and movement direction of the player's hand.

3
Controls the In-Game Player

Hand position is mapped to the on-screen catcher. Move your hand left or right to control the player and catch food.

4
Touchless Interaction

No keyboard, no mouse, no joystick — pure AI-powered gesture control makes gameplay immersive and accessible for all ages.

🤖✋

AI-Powered Gesture Control

PictoBlox uses machine learning models to detect 21 hand keypoints from your webcam feed. This powers real-time, lag-free motion tracking without any special hardware.
